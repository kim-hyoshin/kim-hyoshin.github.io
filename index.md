**Welcome to my homepage!**

- My research lies at the intersection of **economics of ideas, science, innovation, and AI**. I study how technological change—especially AI—shapes labor markets, human capital investment, and urban development. I also apply machine learning and satellite imagery to measure economic outcomes in hard-to-reach settings.
